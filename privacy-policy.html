We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. Please contact us if you would like to exercise these rights.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@bagnook.com.
